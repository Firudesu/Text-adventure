Setup Wizard - Get started in minutes

Welcome to Game Design Review!

This setup wizard will help you configure your game design review system. You'll be able to:

  • Create and manage design review issues
  • Upload screenshots and videos
  • Draw annotations on images
  • Sync with Google Sheets
  • Assign tasks to team members
  • Track progress and completion

Add Your Team Members

Add the team members who will be working on design reviews. You can always add more later.

Google Sheets Integration (Optional)

Connect to Google Sheets to automatically sync your issues and collaborate with your team. This step is optional - you can skip it and configure later.
